Definition of basketball

Basketball is a game played between two teams of five players each on a rectangular court, usually indoors.
Each team tries to score by tossing the ball through the opponent’s goal, an elevated horizontal hoop and net called a basket.

Rules of the game

The rules governing play of the game are based on Naismith’s five principles requiring a large, light ball,
>handled with the hands;
>no running with the ball;
>no player being restricted from getting the ball when it is in play;
>no personal contact;
>a horizontal, elevated goal.
